Offspring to a duo of half-snake/half-human monster hybrids, Cerberus, aka the "multi-headed hound of hades" is having an existential crisis.

Since birth, Deathby, Snuggles, and Tickles — the individually named heads of Cerberus — were tasked to greet newly dead arrivals into the underworld with puppy-like squee while simultaneously eviscerating anyone who dared an escape attempt. After thousands of years of flawless performance reviews, the lack of appreciation from upper management paired with zero opportunity for advancement has left our trio of antiheroes to ponder...

“What in the actual hell am I are we doing here?”

Forced to reconcile how their early life decisions lead to such middle age drudgery, they quickly discover that it's Brenda — their serpentine tail of pure evil — who's been working to sabotage them all along. Each head of Cerberus has a plan to put a stop to Brenda's evil ways, but but which one will succeed?
